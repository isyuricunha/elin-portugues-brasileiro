{topic, Elin Update Information}
■ 3/21 2026 EA 23.287 Stable

After updating the stable version, Mods from previous versions may stop working or behave strangely. If you notice unusual behavior, please try disabling Mods and test again.

[Highlights]
* Added the Roster Board. Party members can now be selected from a list of residents across all bases, and party setups can be saved and recalled.
* Mad Millionaires can now appear at a low chance when towns are regenerated.
* Your beloved will send you a letter one year after a proposal.
* Items can now be purchased from the Keeper of the Garden using influence.
* Changed the cherry blossom colors in Mifu Village to the autumn style. Also changed the color of flower petals while worshipping Kizuami to the autumn style.
* Dramatically improved the processing speed of list-type UIs that display NPCs, such as the Resident Board and Roster Board.
* Updated and enhanced the Mod framework. Thanks DK!

[New Content]
* Added a "Launch Console" shortcut to the key settings in the Settings window.
* Added a Kumiromi music box track.
* Added the ability "Money Slap".
* Added the ability "Oren Toss".
* Added the ability "Money Dump".
* Added sound effects to the ability "Draw". Also added several magic sound effects for buffs and similar effects.
* Added the material "Cloud".
* Added the item "Lost Wedding Ring Notice".
* Added the item "★Garokk's Hammer". Added as a Small Medal prize (limited stock; more stock may be added later through quests).
* Added the item "Scroll of Silver Cat Migration". Added to the Keeper's stock.
* Added the item "Scroll of Epithet". Added to the Keeper's stock.
* Added the item "Scroll of Family Tree Forgery". Added to the Keeper's stock.
* Added "Cloud" furniture.
* Added 8 types of "Garden Stone" furniture.
* Added "Roster Board" furniture.
* Added a wall-mounted version of the "Roster Board".
* Added the map object "Stepping Stone".
* Added the tile "Cloud Floor".
* Added the tile "Cloud Block".
* Added the tile "Alien Block".
* Added the tile "Alien Floor".
* Added the tile "Mushroom Floor" (previously existed only as a platform).
* Added 1 neutral NPC (Mad Millionaire).
* Added the achievement "Heh heh heh...".
* Added the achievement "Ah, the Scent of This Herb".
* Added the achievement "I-It's Not Like I'm Expecting Anything from You or Anything!".
* Added the achievement "My Name Is Onev, Mother of All Living Things in Ylva".
* Added the achievement "*Rin*".
* Added the achievement "There, there~ I'll pat you!".
* Added the achievement "This Isn't My Real Big Brother!!!".
* Added and replaced the icon for the achievement "Eh...? No...".

[Additions and Changes]
* Entering Little Garden or the Garden of Eternity now displays the total number of Little Sisters and Silver Cats on the map.
* Harvesting crops for harvest requests now grants Weight Lifting experience to party members based on the number and weight of the crops.
* Weddings can now be held again after remarrying.
* Nearby sounds now still play even while the PC is blind.
* The PC can no longer kiss themselves. If the PC has a pet currently being ridden (parasitizing), targeting yourself will instead kiss that pet.
* During intimate actions or shearing, not only companions but also their minions will now refrain from attacking the PC's target.
* Some abilities, such as Charge, are no longer affected by gene replacement from slime predation and will not be forgotten once learned. This is now also stated in the tooltip when playing as a slime.
* Slightly improved and fixed the underground map of the Derphy brothel.
* Marked some characters such as Billy and Renton as unique characters internally, where they had not previously been flagged as such.
* Reading a Scroll of Faith now raises Piety only when Piety is lower than the Faith skill.
* Steam achievements are no longer checked while the game is being played offline.
* Expanded the character limit for in-game chat. Multi-line input is now split and output line by line. Decorative tags are now also removed from text shown in the log.
* When playing as a Slime, spells with depleted stock are now still shown in the ability list if they have been learned as abilities.
* The "Peaky" feat can now appear on the Horse race at a low chance.
* Added "a large bonus to riding experience" to the effects of the "Peaky" feat.
* The feat "Magic Mastery" can no longer be obtained through genes.
* Added a non-inheritable-on-crafting tag to the trait "Eco".
* Even when the "Automatically Continue Performing" option is enabled, performing will now stop if the character is suffocating.
* The console command "Stats" now displays the total number of migrated Little Sisters and Silver Cats.
* Changed the console commands "ChangeJob" and "ChangeRace" into cheat commands.
* Updated the snow graphics.
* Updated the Chinese translation.

[Fixes]
* Fixed an issue where switching equipment sets could sometimes cause an error.
* Fixed overflows in multiple processes.
* Fixed the red book "About the Foxfolk" placed in Mifu Village being incorrectly flagged as NPC-owned.
* Fixed various other minor issues and made small adjustments.

[Notes]
* The Roster Board turned into a fairly large update, so this Stable release is coming a little earlier than usual.
* Mod support features are currently being heavily enhanced by DK. Going forward, we'd also like to improve compatibility with future base-game version updates where possible.
* As for the Keeper of the Garden's prizes, since catching Silver Cats can be tedious, the current plan is to keep them limited to achievement-style rewards and certain furniture, so that collecting them does not become necessary.
* As for Garokk's Hammer, a way to obtain it again through quests or similar is planned.
* For fixed seeds used by Garokk's Hammer and similar features, I am considering adding an option under "World's Law" to disable fixed seeds.

{topic, Elin Update Information}
■ 4/11 2026 EA 23.295 Stable

After updating the stable version, Mods from previous versions may stop working or behave strangely. If you notice unusual behavior, please try disabling Mods and test again.

[Highlights]
* Traveling Merchants can now occasionally appear on the global map. Their inventory now varies by merchant type. Traveling Merchants are also now guaranteed to appear on festival maps for each season.
* A phantom killer can now rarely appear when towns are regenerated.
* It is now possible to disguise yourself as a baby. While in baby form, the dialogue option "Mommy!" will now appear in conversations with NPCs.
* You can now become a mommy when talking to baby NPCs or NPCs in a baby state.
* Added beastfolk races such as Ratkin and Ravian.
* When you have an Advanced Adventurer License, enemy levels in global map symbol encounters now scale with your fame.
* Buffed Yevan's boon, Flames of Strife.
* Expanded and improved mod-related features, and fixed existing bugs.

[New Content]
* Added the race "Ratkin."
* Added the race "Ravian."
* Added the race "Werewolf."
* Added the feat "False Face."
* Added the feat "Killing Instinct." Granted to some NPCs.
* Added the feat "Missionary of Love."
* Added the feat "Sad Creature."
* Added the ability "Human Guise."
* Added the status "Human Guise." Also made various adjustments to the "Mimic" status.
* Added Red Book "Adventurer's Weekly Issue 18: Party Management."
* Added Red Book "Adventurer's Weekly Issue 19: Vampires." Added to Moyer's stock.
* Added Red Book "Adventurer's Weekly Issue 20: Recycling." Added to Moyer's stock.
* Added the item "Roster Ledger." Added to Loytel's stock. Also adjusted the prices of Loytel's wares.
* Added the item "Mother Milk" (experimental).
* Added the item "Potion of Degeneration." Now sold by Rodwyn.
* Added the furniture "Jure Statue (Colossus)."
* Added 3 types of furniture "Speaker."
* Added the furniture "Stage Platform."
* Added the furniture "Alien Door." Recipe also added.
* Added 2 types of furniture "Candy Decoration."
* Added 2 types of furniture "Spotlight."
* Added the instrument "Harmonica." Recipe also added.
* Added exclusive performance tracks for the "Harmonica."
* Added variations to the tile "Alien Block."
* Added the tile "Alien Block (Different from the Above)."
* Added 3 types of tile "Alien Wall."
* Added 3 types of tile "Alien Pillar."
* Added 3 types of tile "Alien Floor." Added squishy footstep sounds.
* Added 2 types of tile "Stage Floor."
* Added 4 NPCs.
* Added 2 monsters.

[Additions and Changes]
* Set the maximum level of Yevan's boon "Flames of Strife" to 5. Its effects have been changed to physical damage increase and physical damage cap increase. The tooltip now also shows the current effects more clearly.
* When "Flames of Strife" is level 1 or higher, its SP drain effect now gradually increases.
* When "Flames of Strife" is level 2 or higher, it can now spread to allies.
* When "Flames of Strife" is level 3 or higher, bleed resistance now gradually increases.
* Neutral NPCs can now spawn randomly on roadside outdoor maps.
* Added a cap to the potential gained from succubus play and baby play, since using baby play on NPCs such as the Garden Master could instantly max it out.
* You no longer gain experience toward potential if you die during succubus play or baby play.
* While worshipping Horome, anyone can now become your mama. Also, Horome followers no longer become a Sad Creature even if they repeat baby play.
* NPCs generated on the global map are now displayed as "???" by name (experimental, since the actual NPC is generated during the symbol encounter).
* Unique monsters and similar NPCs no longer generate randomly on the global map.
* If an NPC generated in a symbol encounter has the Baby feat, that NPC will no longer scale based on the PC's fame level in some cases.
* Repeatedly choosing "I'm going to attack you" when talking to travel merchants now lowers karma instead of raising it.
* Slightly adjusted the Strength values of some non-playable races.
* NPCs no longer buy or sell on user maps.
* Added Cooking as a starting skill for the race "Juere."
* Added Wolf Tail as a rare drop for NPCs of the race "Werewolf."
* Gave the feat "Sad Creature" to the NPC "Raphael."
* Adjusted the prices of the Keeper of the Garden's goods.
* Added "Cloud" to the Keeper of the Garden's stock, and changed the stock count of each item to 99.
* Added the various god colossi to the Keeper of the Garden's stock.
* The item "Incubator" can now also be used inside tents.
* Decorative signs can now also be used inside tents, and their designs can be changed.
* Signboards such as the dining sign can now also function inside tents.
* In the widget "Status Info," the direction in which text and icons are displayed now changes depending on the pivot position.
* (English version) Changed the name of "resident list" to "resident ledger."
* Updated the Chinese translation.

[Mod-Related] (Thanks to DK)
* Expanded mod functionality related to textures and sprites.
* Added snow texture support for custom skins.
* Added animation support for custom sprites.
* Fixed the behavior when uploading mods while playing on a Steam Family account.
* Fixed an issue where the names of subscribed mods could sometimes fail to be retrieved from Steam.
* Fixed issues related to language mods.
* Other mod-related feature additions and fixes.

[Fixes]
* Fixed an issue where stamina drain and mana drain attack effects were not being applied to unarmed attacks.
* Fixed an issue where some items, such as a lovingly made lunchbox, could not be placed on the ground.
* Fixed an issue where the target of Telekinesis would change if a mana warning dialog appeared while using Telekinesis.
* Fixed an issue where the effect of the Ceasefire Whistle could wear off when going to sleep.
* Fixed some lines in the story where Kettle was described as seeing things with his eyes even though he is blind. Also fixed some typos in the Palmia Times.
* Fixed several processing overflows.
* Other minor fixes and adjustments.

[Notes]
* It is intended that karma increases when choosing "I'm going to attack you" on traveling merchants, since it is better manners than attacking without warning.
* For phantom killers, more gimmicks and related features may be added by the next stable release.
* The skills and abilities of the newly added beastfolk races are still planned to be adjusted for a while.

■ 3/2 2026 EA 23.282 Stable
(Translated by ChatGPT)

[Additions and Changes]
* Added a chat key to key settings (default: Return Key). Pressing it in-game allows the PC to speak any line of dialogue.
* Entering @ followed by a number (e.g., @1) at the start of chat now makes another party member speak the line.
* Entering "nyan" in chat now displays the current number of cats lured with Churyu. Also improved how cats lured with Churyu are counted.
* Enter and Backspace can now be assigned in key settings.
* Wedding rings and collars given during a proposal are now upgraded to mythical quality (no additional enchants; mainly for destruction protection. Existing ones will change upon reforging).
* "Intimate" actions with drunk characters or a fiancé/fiancée can now be performed inside tents.
* Changed the default material of the furniture "Champagne Tower" to Topaz.
* Renamed the ability "French Kiss" to "Deep Kiss."
* Added Chinese achievement text.

[Fixes]
* Fixed an issue where underwater effects would appear during wedding dialogue if the ceremony quest was initiated underwater.
* Other minor fixes and adjustments.

[Notes]

* The quality change for wedding rings is somewhat experimental. For the next stable version, considering introducing Garok’s Hammer (which would add enchants in addition to upgrading quality).

* Renamed the action "Pee" to "Bathe."
* Wandering priests can now rarely spawn on outdoor maps.
* Updated the mod framework. Thanks, DK!

[Marriage]
* If you possess a engagement ring or collar, you can now propose to companions via the "Actually…" dialogue option.
* On a successful proposal, a message is sent to the Palmia Times log.
* After your first successful proposal, Mimululu begins selling the Kumiromi and Ehekatl cassette tapes.
* Added the feat "Beloved," granted to fiancés.
* Rings or collars given to a fiancé become attuned to them and can only be equipped by that fiancé. They also gain an additional rune slot.
* Using a Scroll of Reconstruction on an attuned ring or collar will regenerate it as bound again, retaining the rune slot bonus.
* Attuned engagement rings or collars cannot be used for proposals or duplicated.
* Attuned engagement rings or collars cannot be equipped simultaneously in multiples.
* Added new dialogue when engaging in intimate actions with a fiancé. Fiancés no longer charge money for such interactions.
* Marriage history is now displayed on the PC character sheet.
* Fiancés in the same party may fight if there are multiple.
* Added the feat "Flowers in Both Arms." Added as a starting feat for the Eulderna and Juere races, allowing multiple fiancés.
* Added a wedding quest and the Old Church map. You can hold a wedding there by speaking to your fiancé.
* Added the field effect "Bottomless Banquet," always active during weddings.
* Speaking to your fiancé during the wedding grants the weapon "Cake Cutter."
* Added the ability "Kiss," learned upon completing the wedding quest.
* Added new text when speaking to attendees or your fiancé during the wedding.
* Added wedding congratulatory lines from the gods.
* Engagements can now be dissolved by submitting Divorce Papers.

[Slime]
* Adjusted initial skills and stats for the Slime race.
* Added the feat "Predatory Evolution" to Slimes (effect applies to player only).
* Set the maximum level of "Predatory Evolution" to 8. Gene slot limit starts at -6 and increases with each level. Leveling format is similar to the Fox maid.
* With "Predatory Evolution," eating genes directly grants their effects, consuming feat points based on gene cost.
* Required feat points for gene consumption reduced to 1/20 of original gene cost.
* Eating a recessive gene removes the most recently acquired gene. Eating a brain gene shuffles all acquired genes.
* Added the ability "Devour," automatically learned when possessing "Predatory Evolution."
* Using "Devour" on a target absorbs its genes without consuming feat points. The target immediately becomes hostile.
* If no valid gene is obtained after multiple rolls, a recessive gene is acquired instead.
* When using "Devour" while not hungry, a message is displayed. On success, grants the same food effect as eating an egg.
* The PC now uses learned abilities in auto-combat. Spells learned via genes do not consume stock.
* Learned abilities gain experience even without stock.
* Some abilities, such as "Hand of Weakening," are now used by the player in auto-combat.
* The "Maconomy" effect now always applies to learned abilities, even without sufficient stock.
* Genes containing the feat "Sacred Twilight" cannot be recovered as recessive genes (PC only), and such genes cannot be absorbed via predation.
* The feat "Fox Maiden" cannot be applied to the PC via genes. The feat "Curse of the Manor" and the ability "Devour" can no longer appear on genes.

[Notes]
* Slime and Predatory Evolution mechanics may continue to change. Some abilities may revert to auto-combat-only use.
* Gene integration for non-Slime PCs may be still implemented in the future. Predatory Evolution lays the groundwork for this system.
* Mimululu cannot currently be invited as a resident. Her cassette shop may require specific quest completion in the future.
* Marriage features will continue to expand. Additional date spot maps and multiple wedding venues may be interesting.. It may even become possible for players to create and upload their own wedding venues.
* A legacy carryover system of Elona is under consideration but would be a long-term update. Rather than passing down genes with your spouse, the idea is for the two of you to cultivate the world’s seeds and saplings together—allowing the world itself (maps and residents you created) to carry over.

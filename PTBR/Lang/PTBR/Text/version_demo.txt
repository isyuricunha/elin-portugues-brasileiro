{topic, Welcome to Elin's Demo!}
Welcome to the Elin Demo!

The content in this demo reflects the state of the game as of December 2, 2024, and is subject to significant updates in the future.

There are limitations in the demo version:

* Players can only earn up to 10 feat points (levels).
* The base level is capped at Level 3.
* Network features are disabled.

Take your time and enjoy exploring the world of Ylva!

<i>※ Please note that Elin is in Early Access and still under heavy development.</i>
